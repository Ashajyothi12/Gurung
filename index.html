About Gurung Bazar

Gurung Bazar is a family-owned business dedicated to providing the highest quality Asian groceries to the St. Louis community. We take pride in offering a wide selection of fresh produce, spices, lentils, sweets, and specialty items that bring the authentic flavors of Asia to your kitchen.

Our knowledgeable staff is always ready to help you find exactly what you need and offer suggestions for your culinary adventures. Visit us today and experience the difference of shopping at a store that cares about quality and customer satisfaction.

Frequently Asked Questions

Forms of payments accepted

You can use PayPal for your payments for online purchases. If ordering by phone you can use major credit cards such as Visa, MasterCard, American Express and Discover for payments.

Shipping Policy

Free Shipping on all orders over $50.

All orders are picked and processed between 2-3 business days. All orders ship from St. Louis. Upon your orders shipment you will be emailed a tracking number to track the shipment. Orders will only ship within the United States. Estimated shipping time is about 5-7 business days.

Shipping costs are based on your total order amount before sales tax and promotional discounts are applied. Prices are per shipping address.

Sales Tax

Sales tax varies by state. For orders from Missouri the sales tax of 4.225% is applied.

Can orders be cancelled?

Orders cannot be cancelled or modified once it has shipped.
